18 U.S.C. § 2257 Compliance Statement

Last Updated: January 2025

All persons depicted in visual content appearing on SIREN were at least 18 years of age at the time of the creation of such depictions.

Compliance Statement

SIREN is committed to full compliance with the requirements of 18 U.S.C. § 2257 and 28 C.F.R. Part 75 (collectively, the "Regulations") regarding the maintenance of records pertaining to the production of visual depictions of actual sexually explicit conduct.

Record-Keeping Requirements

In compliance with the Regulations, SIREN maintains records of all performers appearing in content hosted on our platform. These records include:

  • Legal name and any stage names or aliases
  • Date of birth
  • Government-issued photo identification
  • Proof of age documentation

Secondary Producer

For content not produced by SIREN but hosted on our platform, SIREN acts as a secondary producer. Records are maintained by the original producers and are available through their respective websites and custodians of records. SIREN verifies that all content providers maintain appropriate 2257 documentation before hosting their content.

Exemptions

Some content on SIREN may be exempt from 18 U.S.C. § 2257 record-keeping requirements if it:

  • Was produced before July 3, 1995
  • Does not depict actual sexually explicit conduct as defined by 18 U.S.C. § 2256
  • Falls under other applicable exemptions

Verification of Age

SIREN requires all content producers to verify the age of every performer using valid government-issued photo identification prior to any production. We maintain copies of such identification and associated documentation as required by law.
