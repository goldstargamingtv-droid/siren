Content Complaint Procedure

Last Updated: January 2025

SIREN is committed to maintaining a safe platform. We take all content complaints seriously and have established this procedure for reporting concerns about content on our platform.

üö® Urgent: Report Illegal Content

If you believe content on SIREN depicts illegal activity, including but not limited to child sexual abuse material (CSAM), non-consensual content, or trafficking, please report immediately to:

Email: support@viewsiren.com (Subject: URGENT - Illegal Content)

We review urgent reports within 24 hours and will take immediate action and report to appropriate authorities as required by law.

What You Can Report

You may submit a complaint regarding:

  • Illegal content: Any content that violates local, state, federal, or international law
  • Non-consensual content: Content depicting individuals without their consent
  • Age concerns: Content where a performer appears to be underage
  • Copyright infringement: Content that infringes on your intellectual property (see DMCA Policy)
  • Identity/impersonation: Content using your likeness without permission
  • Terms violations: Content that violates our Terms of Service

How to Submit a Complaint

To report content, please email support@viewsiren.com with the following information:

  1. Your name and contact information
  2. The URL(s) or specific location of the content in question
  3. A clear description of your complaint
  4. The reason you believe the content should be removed
  5. Any supporting documentation (if applicable)
  6. A statement that the information provided is accurate

Our Review Process

  1. Acknowledgment: We will acknowledge receipt of your complaint within 48 hours
  2. Review: Our Trust & Safety team will review the reported content
  3. Investigation: We may request additional information if needed
  4. Action: If the complaint is valid, we will take appropriate action (removal, account suspension, etc.)
  5. Notification: We will notify you of the outcome when possible

Response Times

  • Illegal content reports: Reviewed within 24 hours
  • Non-consensual content: Reviewed within 24-48 hours
  • Copyright complaints: Reviewed within 5 business days
  • Other complaints: Reviewed within 7 business days

Appeal Process

If you are a content creator and your content was removed due to a complaint, you may appeal the decision by contacting support@viewsiren.com with:

  • The content that was removed
  • Reason you believe the removal was in error
  • Any supporting documentation

Content Moderation Policy

SIREN prohibits the following content:

  • Content depicting minors or anyone under 18 years of age
  • Non-consensual sexual content
  • Content depicting actual violence or harm
  • Content promoting illegal activities
  • Content obtained through hacking, theft, or fraud
  • Revenge porn or content shared without performer consent

External Reporting

In addition to contacting SIREN, you may also report illegal content to:

  • NCMEC CyberTipline: CyberTipline.org (for CSAM)
  • FBI IC3: ic3.gov (for internet crimes)
  • Local law enforcement: For emergencies, contact your local authorities
