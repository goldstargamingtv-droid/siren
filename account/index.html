Frequently Asked Questions
How do I cancel my subscription?
You can cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing section. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.
What devices are supported?
SIREN supports most modern web browsers on desktop, mobile, and tablet devices. For VR content, we support major VR headsets including Meta Quest, Valve Index, and HTC Vive.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, all payment processing is handled by secure, PCI-compliant payment processors. We never store your full credit card details on our servers.
How do I download content for offline viewing?
Downloads are available on Premium and VIP plans. Look for the download icon on supported content, and it will be saved to your Downloads section.
